Honey-Garlic Chicken with Broccoli & Sweet Potato Mash

Home

Ingredients

  • 1-2 chicken breasts
  • 1 frozen baked sweet potato
  • 1 cup broccoli florets (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 tsp olive oil (or other light oil)
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • ½ tsp honey (optional for sweetness)
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: paprika or chili flakes for a light kick

Instructions

  1. Prepare chicken
    1. Pat the chicken dry and season with salt and pepper.
    2. Heat a nonstick pan with olive oil over medium heat.
    3. Add garlic, sauté until fragrant (about 30 seconds).
    4. Add chicken breasts and cook for 6-7 minutes per side until cooked through.
    5. Add soy sauce and honey near the end, letting the glaze coat evenly.
  2. Cook broccoli
    1. While the chicken cooks, steam or microwave broccoli until bright green and tender (about 2–3 minutes).
    2. Sprinkle a tiny bit of salt or drizzle with leftover soy–garlic glaze from the pan.
  3. Warm sweet potato
    1. Microwave the frozen baked sweet potato for 3–5 minutes until hot and soft.
    2. Mash it with a fork, adding a pinch of salt or a tiny drizzle of olive oil.
  4. Assemble
    1. Spread the mashed sweet potato on a plate.
    2. Slice the chicken and place it on top.
    3. Arrange broccoli on the side and pour any remaining glaze from the pan over everything.

Optional upgrade:

Add a squeeze of lemon or a sprinkle of sesame seeds over the broccoli for brightness.

This version keeps everything in nutritional harmony — complex carbs for steady energy, lean protein for muscle repair, and broccoli to round out the micronutrients.
